### Mechanisms of Resistance

The mechanisms of resistance to ALS inhibitors in Phelipanche ramosa are similar to those observed in other weed species. These include:

1. **Target Site Mutations**: Mutations in the ALS gene can alter the binding site of the herbicide, reducing its efficacy. These mutations are often point mutations that confer a high level of resistance ([Ephrath et al., 2012](https://doi.org/10.1614/WS-D-11-00070.1)).
